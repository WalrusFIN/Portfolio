About Me

Some information about my work experience and studies

BIO

I am a 23-year-old English language expert from Jyväskylä, Finland. During my studies at the University of Jyväskylä I majored in English language while also studying information systems and information technology as minor subjects. In 2017 I also spent a semester studying in the Netherlands as an exchange student. Both my language and IT-skills were highly useful in my previous job as a translator for a Finnish IT-company. I am aiming to be employed in a position where I can utilise both my language and IT-skills, as well as my international experience.

Translator, 6.2019 - 1.2020

Samlink

During my time at Samlink I translated various materials from Finnish to English. The materials included test cases for testing netbanking services and various other texts related to both IT and finance. In this position I was given a great deal of responsibility in carrying out my translations, which required great attention to detail and good time management skills.

Festival volunteer, 06.2019

Midnight Sun Film Festival

I took part in organising the film festival as a volunteer in Sodankylä, Finland. During the festival I learned how a large international event such as the festival is organised from the point-of-view of the organiser, while on my free-time I was able to attend the festival. My tasks also included customer service and cashier work.

Translator trainee, 2.2018 - 4.2018

Finnish Ski Association

In this short internship I translated the Finnish Ski Associations instructional materials used for organising outdoors events for children and elementary school students. During this internship I learned professional communication when interacting with my supervisor as well as how to translate Finnish-language texts into English.
